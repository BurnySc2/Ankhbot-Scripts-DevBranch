### HowTo and Links:

First create an Application with the Redirect URI: http://localhost
Then go to the OAuth Link and replace "CLIENTID" in the Link with the ClientID you'll get after creating the Application.

### ClientID:
https://www.twitch.tv/kraken/oauth2/clients/new

### OAuth: 
https://api.twitch.tv/kraken/oauth2/authorize?response_type=token&client_id=CLIENTID&redirect_uri=http://localhost&scope=channel_editor&scope=user_read+channel_editor

### RankedFTW Link:
http://rankedftw.com
Search for your Accountname e.g. "Brain" or "Burny" and then click on your profile and the Ladder-Icon top left. 
It will be a link like this: "http://www.rankedftw.com/ladder/lotv/1v1/mmr/?team=1340658"
